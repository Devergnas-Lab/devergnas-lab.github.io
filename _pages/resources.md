## Open Science

The Devergnas Lab is committed to open and reproducible neuroscience.  
Whenever possible, we share tools, analysis code, and datasets developed in the lab.

---

## Hardware and 3D-Printed Tools

Members of the lab design and build custom hardware for electrophysiology experiments, including specialized tools for nonhuman primate recordings.

These designs are shared to facilitate reproducibility and to support other laboratories developing similar experimental systems.

### Custom Electrophysiology Tools

**3D-Printed Electrode Holder**

Custom holder designed for stable positioning of electrodes during chronic recording experiments.

Links

- CAD files  
- STL files  
- Assembly documentation  

**Specialized Recording Components**

Additional custom components developed in the lab include:

- electrode positioning fixtures  
- telemetry mounting components  
- experimental rig adaptations  

Design files will be shared as they become available.

## Analysis Code

Analysis pipelines developed in the lab are shared through GitHub repositories.

Examples include:

**Seizure Dynamics Analysis**

Computational tools for analyzing neural dynamics in seizure recordings.

**Neuromodulation Experiments**

Analysis tools for stimulation experiments and electrophysiological recordings.
