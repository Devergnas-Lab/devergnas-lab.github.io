## Prospective Students and Trainees

The Devergnas Lab welcomes inquiries from students and trainees interested in
epilepsy, neuromodulation, sleep, and systems neuroscience.

Prospective students, postbaccalaureate researchers, and other interested
trainees are encouraged to reach out by email with a brief statement of
interest and a current CV.

Please include:
- a short description of your research interests
- your current position or training stage
- your CV
